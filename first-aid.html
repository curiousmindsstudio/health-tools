🩹 First Aid Guide

Learn essential first aid techniques and medicines to handle common emergencies. Always call emergency services in severe situations.

Basic First Aid Kit

  • Adhesive bandages / sterile gauze
  • Antiseptic wipes / cream
  • Painkillers (Paracetamol)
  • Antihistamines
  • Thermometer, tweezers, scissors
  • Elastic bandage / cold pack

Common Emergencies

Bleeding / Cuts

Apply pressure with a clean cloth or gauze. Clean wound with antiseptic. Cover with a bandage. Seek medical help if bleeding is heavy.

Burns

Cool the burn under running water for 10–20 mins. Cover with sterile, non-stick dressing. Avoid applying butter or oil.

Fractures / Sprains

Immobilize the injured area with splint or bandage. Apply cold packs to reduce swelling. Seek medical attention immediately.

Choking

Encourage coughing. Perform Heimlich maneuver if person cannot breathe. Call emergency services immediately.

Fainting

Lay the person flat and raise legs. Keep airway clear. Loosen tight clothing. Seek medical help if unconscious for more than a minute.
